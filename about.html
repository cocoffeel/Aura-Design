I'm Laura Liu

A Graphic Designer and Brand Strategist in Vancouver, BC

I am a passionate graphic designer with a solid problem-solving and project-planning background. My love for illustration and motion graphics has added more depth to my creative toolkit.

Before transitioning to graphics, I worked as a product manager for mass-market mobile products, where I learned the value of collaboration across teams—from designers and developers to marketers. From pure passion and boldness, I started my own art print brand—a leap of faith that let me explore visual storytelling in its most personal form. Building the brand taught me about consistency, creativity, and how design and marketing can work together to tell compelling stories.

I believe curiosity, critical thinking, and focus on details really make a difference. I'll be updating my projects regularly—if you have any questions or just want to connect, don't hesitate to reach out!
