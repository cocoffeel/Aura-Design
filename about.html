I'm Laura Liu

A Graphic Designer and Brand Strategist in Vancouver, BC

I am a passionate graphic designer with a solid problem-solving and project-planning background. My interests and experiences in illustrations and motion graphics have further enriched my skill set. Before transitioning to graphics, I served as a product manager for mass-market mobile products, where I honed my collaborative skills with designers, developers, and marketing. I've also established my art print design brand, creating visually consistent assets for both web and print. This experience has reinforced my belief as a lifelong creative, showing how design and marketing can work together to tell compelling stories. I believe curiosity, critical thinking, and focus on details can make a difference. I'll be updating my projects regularly—if you have any questions or just want to connect, don't hesitate to reach out!
