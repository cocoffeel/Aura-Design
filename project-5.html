San Antonio Spurs Mascot

Mascot Design

Feb 2024

The objective of this project is to redesign a mascot for the NBA team, the San Antonio Spurs. It should reflect the team's spirit and essence while embracing simplicity, a distinct color palette, and symbolic elements. Key factors include visual appeal, originality, and versatility across various digital and print media platforms.

Dunitoni

The Grace Guardian

The name is in honor of Spurs’legendary trio of Tim Duncan, Tony Parker, and Manu Ginóbili.


I selected the coyote as the mascot, inspired by its prevalence in San Antonio and its current representation for the team. Retaining the memorable and symbolic green color, I have infused a friendly and elegant demeanor into the design. The intention is to accentuate the team's core values: teamwork, discipline and resilience.


I've carefully chosen a complementary color scheme that revolves around the dominant hues of dark yellow or orange. This choice, reminiscent of the earthy tones, symbolizes the unwavering reliability of our team. To infuse a touch of creativity, I've incorporated a small area of vibrant green, adding a dynamic and innovative element to the overall aesthetic.
