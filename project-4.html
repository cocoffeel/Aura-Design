San Diego Latino Film Festival

Poster

Oct 2023


Program Used: Photoshop

This project is a proposal for the 31th San Diego Film Festival.

Rationale

In this poster, I aim to capture the essence of the early days of cinema. I've incorporated iconic elements like the historic Orpheum Theatre, an old-fashioned popcorn machine, vintage vehicles, and bustling scenes of people gathering to watch films. Together, these elements transport viewers back in time to the birth of cinema.
