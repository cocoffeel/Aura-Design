SNAP - 5-min Time Capsule

Brand Identity, Poster, Landing Page

April 2024

This project features a promotional campaign for an imaginary brand SNAP, focusing on logo design, color scheme, poster, and landing page.

Introducing SNAP

The product is a time capsule that enables individuals to experience a five-minute journey back in time.


For the logo design, I aim to blend the number "5" with a time travel theme. In the final design, I incorporate an arrow within the "5" shape and create the silhouette of an hourglass in the negative space. Additionally, a leaf shape is incorporated to signify the medicinal aspect. Additionally, the "5" doubles as an "s" in the wordmark "SNAP". The primary challenge lies in maintaining the integrity of the "5" shape while subtly suggesting an "s" shape without causing confusion.


Using a blue monochrome color scheme, the logo evokes feelings of reliability, safety, and technology.

Poster & Landing Page

For the poster, I utilize a sequence of "5" to visually represent the product's ability to divide time into clips. The landing page inherits the visual elements from the poster, with the shape of a reversed hourglass echoing the silhouette of the logo.
